Popular Destinations

In India

Agra

Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Leh-Ladakh

Breathtaking landscapes, high-altitude lakes, and thrilling road trips.

Kerala

Peaceful houseboat rides in Alleppey, lush green tea gardens in Munnar, and beautiful beaches in Varkala.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Pristine white-sand beaches, scuba diving spots, and crystal-clear waters.

Abroad

Kyoto

Kyoto is a cultural treasure trove, known for its ancient temples, tranquil gardens, and stunning traditional architecture.

Santorini, Greece

Santorini is a breathtaking island with whitewashed buildings, crystal-clear waters, and mesmerizing sunsets.

New Zealand

New Zealand is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, offering dramatic landscapes, adventure sports, and vibrant nature.

Rome, Italy

Rome is a historical marvel, home to ancient ruins, stunning architecture, and a rich cultural heritage.
